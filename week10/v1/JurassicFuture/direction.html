How to get here FAQ

Driving to Jurassic Future

Jurassic Future is located in the C3 biodome on Earth’s Keiper Ring.

  • The nearest on-ramp is in Quito, Ecuador
  • Follow E28B West toward Panamericana Nte.
  • At the roundabout, take the third exit for the KR69 expressway East
  • Follow signs for C3 Biodome exit
  • Sub-Ring parking is available

Taking Public Transportation to Jurassic Future

Many forms of public transportation are available, depending on your ticket type:

  1. Bus transit on the Magenta line is included with all pass types
  2. Space elevator access is included with Galactic, Univeral, and Multiversal passes
  3. Teleport pod access is included with Multiversal passes

Flight to Jurassic Future

Whether you’re flying from Earth or beyond, we’ve got you covered!

  • The nearest spaceport to the C3 Biodome is Tomas Nooley Memorial Spaceport (KRC2)
  • If you prefer to drive along the scenic Keiper Ring, you can land at the Mariscal Sucre Quito International Airport (UIO)
